**Information Collection and Use**

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as

*   Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
*   The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
*   The time spent on the Application
*   The operating system you use on your mobile device

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

The Application uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to enhance user experience and provide certain features. Specifically, the Application uses Google Gemini to power the AI budget allocation feature. When you use this feature, your spending category names and monthly income figure are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate budget recommendations. All AI processing is performed in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable laws. If you have questions about the AI features or data processing, please contact the Service Provider.

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to email, userID. As a personal finance application, the Application also collects and stores financial data you enter, including but not limited to transaction amounts, account names and balances, income figures, budget allocations, savings goals, and spending categories. The information that the Service Provider requests will be retained by them and used as described in this privacy policy.

The Application may request permission to send you push notifications for transaction reminders. If you grant this permission, the Application schedules local notifications on your device. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

If you choose to enable biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint), the Application stores your login credentials securely on your device using the operating system's secure storage. Biometric data itself is processed entirely by your device's operating system and is never transmitted to the Service Provider or any third party.
